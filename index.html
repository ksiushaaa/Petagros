Who We Are

Moldova's leading
PET preform enterprise

Petagros-Com S.R.L. is a Moldovan company specialising exclusively in the production of PET preforms — the foundational component of plastic packaging for the beverage and food industries.

In 2003, we built the first modern PET preform manufacturing facility in Moldova. Since then, we have grown into the country's leading producer, investing continuously in capacity, quality, and technology.

Headquartered in Ungheni, Moldova, our facility operates with advanced injection moulding lines and serves clients across the beverage and brewing sectors.

Our investment program — exceeding €8.5 million — has expanded annual production from 156 million to 300 million preforms, with a production capacity of over 600 million preforms per year.

2003
Moldova's First Modern Preform Factory Company founded and first-of-its-kind PET preform plant constructed in Ungheni.
